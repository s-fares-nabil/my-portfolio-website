Credentials

Certification Roadmap

Ordered from entry-level to professional, with estimated costs and timelines.

1

MATLAB Onramp & Control Design Onramp

MathWorks · Free

Interactive 2-hour introductions. Covers MATLAB basics, Simulink, and feedback control design. Prerequisite for any controls role.

Year 1 Summer ~4 hours total Free
2

ARM Cortex-M Architecture & Programming

ARM Education · Free

ARM's official MOOCs covering Cortex-M0/M4 architecture, memory maps, interrupt handling, and CMSIS programming. Directly applicable to STM32 work.

Year 1-2 ~20 hours Free
3

STM32 MOOCs (Nucleo Board)

STMicroelectronics · Free

Official STM32 courses: GPIO, timers, ADC, DMA, UART/SPI/I2C using STM32CubeMX and HAL. Hands-on with Nucleo boards.

Year 2 ~30 hours Free (hardware ~$15)
4

Siemens Certified Programmer — TIA Portal

Siemens SITRAIN · ~$400-600

Industry-standard certification for Siemens PLC programming (S7-1200/1500). Ladder logic, FBD, STL, HMI basics, PROFINET. Highly valued in Egypt.

Year 2-3 ~2-3 months prep $400-600
5

ISA CCST Level 1

International Society of Automation · ~$300

Certified Control Systems Technician — validates instrumentation, calibration, loop checking, and troubleshooting skills. Good entry-level automation credential.

Year 3 ~3 months study ~$300
6

ISA CAP Associate

ISA · ~$300-400

Certified Automation Professional entry pathway. Validates automation lifecycle knowledge: feasibility, definition, design, implementation, operations. Can count as 1 year of experience toward full CAP.

Year 4 ~3-4 months study ~$300-400

Project Pipeline

Specific projects to build each year that demonstrate real automation & embedded skills.

Year 1

DC Motor Speed Controller

Arduino + L298N H-bridge + potentiometer. Measure RPM with IR sensor, display on LCD. First closed-loop control experience.

Year 2

STM32 CAN Bus Sensor Node

Nucleo board reading a sensor, sending data over CAN bus, receiving on a second board. Demonstrates industrial communication protocol.

Year 3

PLC-Based Conveyor Sorter

Siemens S7-1200 + HMI + sensors. Sort objects by height/material. SCADA dashboard showing throughput, rejects, OEE. Real automation cell.

Year 4

Capstone: Industrial Automation Cell

PLC + SCADA + robot arm (simulated or real) + conveyor. MES integration. Safety circuit. Full Industry 4.0 demo. This is the portfolio centerpiece.
